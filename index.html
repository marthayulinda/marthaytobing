KP & MBKM Website

19 November 2024 - December 2024

Informatics Department, Institut Teknologi Del

This website, built with Laravel 11, is a web-based information system to streamline the Internship (KP) and Independent Learning (MBKM) processes for the Informatics Department at Institut Teknologi Del. This system allows students to easily access requirements, submit feasibility and registration forms, and receive real-time notifications on their application status. It features a dashboard displaying student distribution data and supports role-based access for students, KP/MBKM coordinators, the Head of the Department, and academic advisors. Coordinators can review applications and make approval decisions, while the Head of the Department and academic advisors can provide additional notes to support decision-making. Designed to be integrated, efficient, and user-friendly, this system enhances accessibility and simplifies the entire KP and MBKM process.
